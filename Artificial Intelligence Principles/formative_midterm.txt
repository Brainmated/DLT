Transform the following sentences into Logic statements
a.	If someone drives a car and is drunk, then this person breaks the law.
b.	John drives his Mercedes.
c.	Mercedes is a car.
d.	John is drunk.

p = drives a car
q = is drunk
r = breaks the law

E(x)(p(x) ^ q(x)) -> r
